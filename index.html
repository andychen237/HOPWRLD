Problem Space and Design Focus

The pandemic has changed the landscape of college education, effectively changing campus life as we know it. With the move to completely remote education, most of our academic and social life is based on some online meeting platform. However, these platforms were not specifically designed for supporting our daily quarantine life, and with this "new normal", there still remains a significant gap between studying and socializing virtually and physically. Whether it's Zoom fatigue or cabin fever, being a student during these trying times is certainly tough. Therefore, we introduce a brand-new VR platform, hop WRLD, that will expand beyond the walls of a classroom to provide an immersive, interactive environment to better engage college students academically and socially. We believe that with the VR platform, all of us can not only learn better, but also live a better life during this unprecedented time..
